Tall Athlete Tips (1.97m)
🏋️
Core: Your long torso needs a "braced" core. Every rep in the gym is an ab rep. Focus on a flat stomach by engaging the transverse abdominis.
⬇️
Burpees: Avoid jumping out. Step back, lay down, step up. At your height, this saves massive amounts of heart rate energy.
💪
Chest Growth: Use a full range of motion. Because your arms are long, the stretch at the bottom of a DB Press is where the growth happens.

Training Plan
Months 1–4: Aerobic Base & Structural Integrity. Hit all gym reps and stay at Easy pace on runs. Aim for body recomposition. Goal: build your engine and protect joints.
Months 5–8: Power & Threshold. Increase gym weights progressively. Introduce 1 Threshold run per week. Goal: Sub-30min 5K and higher power output.
Months 9–10: HYROX Specificity. Introduce "Compromised Running" (running immediately after leg exercises). Focus on Sled Push and Wall Ball technique. Race mode.
